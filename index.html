How it works

Two ways to get your car fixed

Option 1

Mobile service we come to you

Perfect if you don't want to sit in a waiting room. We come to your home, job, or parking lot with the tools and parts needed to get the job done on site.

Option 2

Drop-off

Prefer the best price? Bring your car to us. Same quality work, lower labor rate since we’re not traveling. Great for bigger jobs and flexible schedules.

Who we are

CJ’s is two car guys who care

CJ’s Mobile Mechanics was created by two friends who’ve been working on cars together since middle school. From driveway fixes to late night repairs for friends and family, turning wrenches has always been our thing. After years of helping everyone around us keep their cars running, we decided it was time to take it seriously and turn that experience into a real business.

We’re hands on mechanics who actually enjoy this work. Our goal is simple: fix your car the right way, explain what we’re doing, and charge a fair price for the time and skill involved.

When you book with CJ’s, you’re dealing directly with the people who are touching your car not a service writer behind a counter.
